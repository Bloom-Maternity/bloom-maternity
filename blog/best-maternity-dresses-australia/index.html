The 12 Best Maternity Dresses in Australia for 2026

2 April 2026 · 8 min read

A curated guide to the most flattering, comfortable and stylish maternity dresses available right now, tested and reviewed for Australian conditions.

With so many maternity dress options available in Australia, narrowing down the best can feel overwhelming. We have tested, researched and reviewed dozens of maternity dresses from Australian and international brands to bring you this definitive guide to the twelve best maternity dresses you can buy in 2026. Every dress on this list has been chosen for its combination of fit, comfort, fabric quality, versatility and value for money.

Best Overall: Ripe Maternity Embrace Dress

The Ripe Maternity Embrace Dress takes the top spot for its exceptional versatility and flattering fit across all three trimesters. Made from a soft bamboo-blend jersey, it features subtle ruching at the sides that accommodates your growing bump while creating a streamlined silhouette. The V-neckline is universally flattering and doubles as nursing-friendly access postpartum. Available in six colours, this dress works equally well for a weekend brunch, office meeting or casual dinner. The bamboo fabric is thermoregulating and incredibly soft against sensitive pregnancy skin.

Best Wrap Dress: Bae the Label Original Wrap

Bae the Label has perfected the maternity wrap dress with their Original Wrap. The adjustable tie means this dress literally grows with you from conception to postpartum, making it exceptional value despite the premium price point. The midi length hits at the most flattering point on the calf, and the fabric has enough weight to drape beautifully without being too heavy for Australian summers. It is available in both solid colours and prints, with new seasonal colourways released regularly.

Best Maxi Dress: Soon Maternity Luna Maxi

For those who love the ease of a maxi dress, the Soon Maternity Luna Maxi delivers on every front. The empire waistline sits above the bump, creating a flowing, elegant line that works for casual and semi-formal occasions. The cotton-blend fabric is lightweight enough for summer but can be layered with a cardigan or jacket for cooler months. The subtle print options are sophisticated rather than overtly maternity, and the dress transitions well to postpartum wear.

Best Budget Option: Cotton On Body Maternity Midi

Proving that great maternity dresses do not require a large budget, the Cotton On Body Maternity Midi offers impressive quality at an accessible price point. The jersey fabric has good stretch and recovery, the side ruching is flattering, and the dress is available in a wide range of colours and prints that change seasonally. It washes well and holds its shape through repeated wear. For those building a maternity wardrobe on a budget, buying two or three of these in different colours provides excellent variety.

Best for Work: Ripe Maternity Layered Knit Dress

The Ripe Maternity Layered Knit Dress bridges the gap between workwear and maternity wear effortlessly. Its structured knit fabric holds its shape throughout a long workday, while the layered design provides discreet nursing access for those planning to return to the office postpartum. The midi length is office-appropriate, and the classic colourways (black, navy, charcoal) work within most corporate dress codes. Pair it with a blazer for meetings or wear it alone for a polished, professional look.

Best Bamboo Dress: Bae the Label Bamboo Everyday

Bamboo fabric has revolutionised maternity wear in Australia, and the Bae the Label Bamboo Everyday dress showcases why. The fabric is incredibly soft, naturally antibacterial and thermoregulating, keeping you comfortable in both air-conditioned offices and outdoor heat. The relaxed fit works from the first trimester through to postpartum, and the simple, clean design means it pairs easily with accessories for different occasions. This is the dress most reviewers say they reach for on a daily basis.

Best for Summer: Angel Maternity Linen Blend Midi

For the Australian summer, the Angel Maternity Linen Blend Midi is hard to beat. The linen-cotton blend is lightweight and breathable, keeping you cool when temperatures soar. The relaxed A-line shape provides plenty of room for your bump without clinging, and the midi length works with both sandals and sneakers. The natural linen texture adds visual interest without bold prints, making this a sophisticated summer option. Be aware that linen wrinkles, though many women find this adds to the relaxed charm of the dress.

Best for Special Occasions: Queen Bee Luxe Wrap Gown

When you need to dress up, the Queen Bee Luxe Wrap Gown delivers genuine glamour. The flowing chiffon outer layer creates movement and elegance, while the stretch jersey lining ensures comfort and a flattering fit. Available in deep jewel tones and classic neutrals, this gown works for weddings, galas and formal dinners. The wrap design means it adjusts to your changing shape and can be worn postpartum with different styling.

Best Nursing-Friendly: Pea in a Pod Dual Purpose Dress

If you want to maximise your investment, the Pea in a Pod Dual Purpose Dress is designed from the ground up for both pregnancy and breastfeeding. Hidden zip nursing access is discreet and practical, while the ruched design flatters the bump during pregnancy and your changing body postpartum. The mid-weight jersey is suitable for most Australian seasons and holds up well to frequent washing. This dress offers genuine dual-purpose value.

Best Bodycon: Ripe Maternity Mia Ruched Dress

For those who want to show off their bump, the Ripe Maternity Mia Ruched Dress is the perfect choice. The all-over ruching creates a figure-hugging fit that celebrates your pregnant shape. The premium stretch fabric is thick enough to smooth rather than cling, and the knee-length cut is versatile for both casual and dressy occasions. This is the dress most commonly recommended for maternity photoshoots due to its flattering, bump-showcasing design.

Best Plus Size Option: Cake Maternity Curve Range

Cake Maternity has expanded their size range to offer genuinely inclusive maternity dresses. Their Curve Range includes dresses up to size 22 that are designed and patterned specifically for larger bodies during pregnancy, rather than simply scaled up from smaller sizes. The fit accounts for different body proportions and provides appropriate support. The designs are stylish and modern, moving away from the limited options that plus-size pregnant women have historically faced in Australia.

Best Casual T-Shirt Dress: Target Maternity Tee Dress

Sometimes the best maternity dress is the simplest one. The Target Maternity Tee Dress is an everyday workhorse that you will reach for repeatedly. The soft cotton jersey is comfortable from morning to night, the relaxed fit works across all trimesters, and the price means you can buy multiples in different colours without guilt. Layer it with a denim jacket or cardigan for cooler days, or wear it alone with sandals in summer. This is the dress that proves great maternity basics do not need to be expensive.

How We Chose These Dresses

Our selection process considered multiple factors including fabric quality, fit across trimesters, versatility, value for money, availability in Australia, size range and real customer feedback. We prioritised dresses that work for multiple occasions and that continue to be useful postpartum. Every dress on this list is currently available for purchase in Australia and has been consistently well-reviewed by pregnant Australian women.
